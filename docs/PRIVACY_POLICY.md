## Overview

Poziomki ("we", "us", "our") is a privacy-focused social application for university students. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

**Key principles:**
- We collect only what's necessary
- We don't sell your data
- We don't show ads
- You can delete your data anytime
- Your data stays in the EU

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### We DO:
- Verify you're a university student
- Match you with users sharing your interests
- Enable messaging between matched users
- Send event notifications you opted into
- Protect the platform from abuse

### We DON'T:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Show you targeted advertisements
- Share data with advertisers
- Use your data for profiling beyond matching
- Track your location continuously

## 5. Data Storage & Security

### Location
All data is stored in the European Union (Hetzner, Germany).

### Security Measures
- TLS 1.3 encryption for all connections
- Passwords hashed with secure algorithms
- Database encryption at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and audit logging

### Chat Encryption
**Current status:** Messages are stored server-side. End-to-end encryption is planned but not yet implemented. See [SECURITY.md](../SECURITY.md) for details.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access** your data | Settings > Privacy > Download my data |
| **Correct** inaccurate data | Edit your profile |
| **Delete** your data | Settings > Account > Delete account |
| **Export** your data | Settings > Privacy > Download my data |
| **Withdraw consent** | Remove optional profile data anytime |
| **Object** to processing | Contact privacy@poziomki.app |
| **Lodge a complaint** | Contact UODO (Polish DPA) |

### Data Deletion
When you delete your account:
- Profile data is deleted immediately
- Messages are anonymized (content retained for other participants)
- Backups are purged within 30 days
- Logs are retained for 30 days for security
