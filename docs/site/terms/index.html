Terms and Community Rules

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Eligibility

Poziomki is intended for university students and related academic communities. Do not use the service if local law prevents you from doing so.

Acceptable use

  • No harassment, threats, stalking, or hate speech.
  • No impersonation or fraudulent activity.
  • No non-consensual sharing of personal data, images, or private messages.
  • No spam, malware distribution, or attempts to disrupt the service.

Moderation

The app includes blocking and reporting flows for conversations and events. Reports may be reviewed manually by the Poziomki team.

Enforcement

Accounts, messages, events, or other content may be limited or removed when they violate these rules or create safety risks.

Support and contact

General support, moderation, and legal questions can be sent to kontakt@poziomki.app.
