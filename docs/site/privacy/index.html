Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Who operates the service

Poziomki is operated by the Poziomki team. Privacy questions can be sent to kontakt@poziomki.app.

Data we process

  • Account data such as email address and password hash.
  • Profile data such as name, bio, photos, university program, and selected interests.
  • Event data such as created events, attendance, approvals, bookmarks, and reports.
  • Messaging data such as direct messages, reactions, typing state, and conversation reports.
  • Operational settings such as privacy toggles, notification preferences, and session state.

Why we process it

  • To provide the app, including login, profiles, events, recommendations, and messaging.
  • To support account security, moderation, and abuse handling.
  • To deliver notifications and synchronize app state across devices.

Analytics and advertising

The current Android codebase does not include third-party analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs.

Retention and deletion

Data is retained while the account is active and for up to 30 days after account deletion where operational cleanup is required. The app includes account export and account deletion tools in the privacy settings.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data or share it with third parties for advertising. Data may be disclosed where required by law.

Your choices

You can export your account data, delete your account, and update privacy controls from within the app.
